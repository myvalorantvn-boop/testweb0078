# Asia One Resort Supply Website

A professional website for Asia One Resort Supply, showcasing hospitality products and services for hotels, restaurants, and guesthouses in Laos.

## Website Structure

### Pages

1. **Homepage (index.html)**
   - Hero section with company slogan
   - Key features and benefits
   - Product categories preview
   - Call-to-action buttons

2. **About Us (about.html)**
   - Company story and establishment
   - Mission and values
   - Company background

3. **Services (services.html)**
   - Full line of hospitality products
   - After-sales service & maintenance
   - Professional installation
   - Customized kitchen design
   - Three-year warranty
   - Lifetime free labour
   - FOOD-SAFE Laos services

4. **Products (products.html)**
   - Complete product catalog with all categories:
     - Kitchen Products
     - Laundry Products
     - Linens
     - Soaps, Spa Products & Room Amenities
     - Furniture
     - E-Transport
     - Food & Beverage
     - Glass Bottling
     - Fitness Equipment
     - BBQ Grills & Smokers
     - Sun & Rain Awnings
     - Tents & Canopies

5. **Our Team (team.html)**
   - Employee development philosophy
   - Training programs
   - Leadership information

6. **Partnerships (partnerships.html)**
   - PURE GLASS BOTTLING SOLUTION
   - LAO NATURAL
   - LAO DISTILLERY COMPANY

7. **Contact (contact.html)**
   - Contact information
   - Contact form
   - Email, WhatsApp, and phone details

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI**: Clean, professional design with smooth animations
- **Easy Navigation**: Dropdown menu for product categories
- **Mobile-Friendly**: Hamburger menu for mobile devices
- **Smooth Scrolling**: Anchor links with smooth scroll behavior
- **Interactive Elements**: Hover effects and animations

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in a web browser to view the homepage
2. Navigate through pages using the menu
3. All pages are linked and functional
4. The contact form can be customized to connect to a backend service
