About Us

Your Partner for Hospitality Products and Solutions

Our Story

Established in 2016, Asia One Supply is your go-to source for quality hospitality products and services nationwide in Laos. From kitchen and laundry items to linens, amenities, and e-transport vehicles, we have everything you need to elevate your search and acquisition.

Our after-sales and installation services ensure that you are always taken care of. With over 40 years of procurement and distribution experience, our Managing Director, Mr. Troy Matusow, and the Asia One team are dedicated to providing competitive pricing and unbeatable value when you purchase locally.

We are proud to support local business and people, and we are committed to helping you succeed.

Our Mission

To provide quality hospitality products and services that help hotels, restaurants, and guesthouses succeed while supporting local communities and businesses in Laos.
